                                        <h1>Welcome to Environmental Response Television.</h1>
                                        <p>Environmental Response Television (ERTV) provides video support to U.S. EPA's Environmental Response Team (ERT).</p>
                                        <p>ERTV responds to request for the ERT to provide broadcast-quality documentary video programs on alternative hazardous waste treatment technologies, unique hazardous waster sites, site investigations, and other ERT activities.   ERTV also produces "The Environmental Response News Magazine, which combines stories and news from the 10 EPA Regions. </p>
                                        <p>ERTV's award winning staff has a combined experience of over 50 years and specializes in communicating complex technical information to a general audience.   State-of-the-art technologies enable ERTV to share media across all digital platforms. ERTV's safety certifications gives it access to sites not available to the mainstream media or independent video production companies. This valuable asset to On-Scene Coordinators and Site Managers can be activated through the ERT Main Office.  Click here to learn more about the ERT and what we do.</p>
                                        <p>These programs are available to public groups, schools, the environmental industry, and government agencies as reference material and teaching tools.  Accessing ERTV’s entire catalog of streaming videos is easy. Simply search for a keyword above to find videos, or click browse all videos below.</p>
